"python3 pyshbench.py x y z"
   - Where x is the binary of the default branch, y is the binary of your patch, and z is the number of trials you want to run.
   - You can also decide to do multiple tests in one run if you want, by adding more group(s) of three fields after x y z.
   - E.g., "python3 pyshbench.py x1 y1 z1 x2 y2 z2".
